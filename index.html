How It Works

Our streamlined 3-step process makes remote hiring simple and effective.

1. Discovery

We learn about your business needs, culture, and specific role requirements to find the perfect match.

2. Vetting

Our rigorous screening process evaluates skills, experience, and cultural fit before presenting candidates.

3. Interview & Hire

You interview pre-vetted candidates and make your selection. We handle all onboarding logistics.

About Bee Productive

Founded in 2020, Bee Productive was born from the realization that small and mid-sized businesses struggle with the time-consuming and expensive process of hiring quality remote talent.

Our mission is simple: to make remote hiring as efficient as a beehive and as simple as honey. We believe in human productivity and focus on creating meaningful connections between businesses and talent.

What sets us apart is our rigorous vetting process and ongoing support to ensure both clients and candidates thrive in their working relationships.
